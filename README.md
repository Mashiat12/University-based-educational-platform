# Universuty based educational platform
Tried to create a platform which can give our students their academic solutions through students who are already expert on that particular course.
## Introduction
University-based educational platform tries to use the power of the internet to develop a
website that can make learning and earning easier for university-going students.It creates
a user friendly communication web portal for providing necessary information and help
through creating a medium between student and tutor.We tried our best to provide a way
to students to find tutor and as well as being a tutor themselves based on their needs.
